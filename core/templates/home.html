About Our Mission

Bridging the Digital Divide in Rural India

Expert Institute is not just a computer center; it is a movement started by Jan Sewa Samiti to ensure that no child remains deprived of modern education due to poverty.

We provide high-quality computer training at minimal costs, helping youth from rural backgrounds gain skills, confidence, and employment opportunities.

A Journey of Hope

Our Origin Story

From One Room to Empowering Thousands

It started in a small, rented room with just two old computers. The founders of Jan Sewa Samiti noticed that while the world was moving towards digitalization, the youth in our villages were being left behind simply because they couldn't afford expensive coaching fees.

Determined to change this, we launched the Expert Institute with a simple mission: "No child should be denied education due to lack of funds."

Initially, we faced many challenges—lack of electricity, resources, and skepticism. But as our first batch of students got jobs and started supporting their families, trust began to build.

Today, we are proud to have trained over 5,000 students. We provide not just computer skills, but also confidence and a path to dignity. Our mission continues until every rural child is digitally literate.
